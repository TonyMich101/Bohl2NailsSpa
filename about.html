Springs' Premier Beauty & Wellness Destination Since 2020

For over five years, Bohl Squared Massage and Nails Lounge has been the cornerstone of luxury and relaxation in Springs, Gauteng. What began as a passion for holistic wellness has blossomed into a sanctuary where hundreds of clients find respite from their busy lives. Our journey is built on a foundation of trust, exceptional skill, and an unwavering commitment to your well-being.

We believe true beauty treatment goes beyond surface-level care. That's why every facial, massage, and nail service is delivered with personalized attention, using premium products and techniques proven to deliver real results. Our dedicated team continuously trains to bring you the latest advancements in spa and beauty therapy, ensuring your experience is nothing short of transformative.

Why We're Different

More Than a Spa – Your Wellness Partner

Choosing Bohl Squared means investing in an experience tailored just for you. We stand apart through our meticulous attention to detail, from the moment you book to the lasting results you enjoy. Our team in Springs doesn't just perform services; we build relationships, taking time to understand your specific needs, whether it's relieving chronic back tension with a deep tissue massage or creating the perfect nail art for a special occasion.

We use only professional-grade, skin-friendly products and our serene environment is designed to be a true escape from the everyday. This dedication to quality and personal care is why we've become the most trusted beauty and wellness destination in Gauteng.
