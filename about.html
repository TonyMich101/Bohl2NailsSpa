Our Journey

Springs' Premier Beauty & Wellness Destination Since 2020

For over five years, Bohl Squared Massage and Nails Lounge has been the cornerstone of luxury and relaxation in Springs, Gauteng. What began as a passion for holistic wellness has blossomed into a sanctuary where hundreds of clients find respite from their busy lives.

We believe true beauty treatment goes beyond surface-level care. That's why every facial, massage, and nail service is delivered with personalized attention, using premium products and techniques proven to deliver real results.
